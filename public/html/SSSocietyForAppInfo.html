About Us

We, at SS Society relentlessly work towards bringing the residential societies and commercial complexes on an online platform. Founded in 2023, Perfect Society was the pioneer in enabling this solution across Pune and later spread its operations to other parts of the country.

Living in a residential society, it is inevitable for residents to get entangled in complex issues. At SocietyRun, we completely understand what it takes to run a housing society seamlessly, thereby making it a hassle-free and easy experience for the residents.

Over the years, we have emerged as a trusted partner by a large number of housing and commercial spaces, in terms of providing automated simplified solutions, thereby delighting many societies. Our motto is to help the inhabitants run their housing and commercial societies easily and accurately.
